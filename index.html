FAQ

Is this okay for complete beginners?

Yes. Sessions are short, scalable, and focus on consistency. Start easy; the point is to show up.

What gear do I need?

Bodyweight is enough. A simple resistance band is a nice bonus for pulls.

How long until I feel results?

Most beginners feel better energy and posture in 2 weeks. Visible changes compound from week 3–8 if you stay consistent.
