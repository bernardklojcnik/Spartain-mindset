8-Week Program Snapshot

Weeks 1–2
Two full-body circuits + brisk walk or light jog; mobility finisher. Learn movement quality.
Weeks 3–4
Progress reps or tempo; add short interval cardio; keep mobility. First Spartan challenge.
Weeks 5–6
Harder variations or added sets; nutrition dial-in (protein + vegetables); sleep routine.
Weeks 7–8
Peak week intensity with smart form; second Spartan challenge; review and set next goals.
